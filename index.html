RETRO FIGHTER BATTLE

HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to RETRO FIGHTER BATTLE! This is a turn-based combat game where two fighters face off in epic combat.

Here's how to get started:

  • Each player chooses a NAME and a VICTORY LINE that will display when they win
  • Each player selects 3 WEAPONS from the available options
  • The total damage potential of your weapons cannot exceed 11 points
  • Players take turns attacking automatically once the battle begins
  • Each fighter starts with 20 HEALTH
  • There's a 20% chance to DODGE any attack!

WEAPONS & COMBAT

Choose your weapons wisely! Each weapon has a different damage value:

KICK
1 DMG
PUNCH
2 DMG
KNIFE
3 DMG
SWORD
4 DMG
GUN
5 DMG

During battle:

  • Weapons are chosen randomly each turn from your selected three
  • The first fighter to reduce their opponent to 0 HEALTH wins
  • The winner's VICTORY LINE will be displayed in glorious retro style!

Ready to battle? Click START to begin!
